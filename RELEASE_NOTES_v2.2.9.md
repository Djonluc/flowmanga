# FlowManga v2.2.9 Release Notes

## 🚀 What's New

### Brand New Sources Added!
We've massively expanded the library of content you can discover and download right from the app. The following sources are now fully integrated and supported:

* **WebNovel (Comics):** You can now browse WebNovel's Trending feeds, read their Latest updates, and search for specific comics directly in the app!
* **ManhuaUS:** Full support added for ManhuaUS, allowing you to seamlessly import and read your favorite manhua.
* **Danbooru & Gelbooru:** For the gallery readers, we've added full integration for Danbooru and Gelbooru, complete with high-quality image fetching.

## 🛠️ Bug Fixes & Improvements

* **Smarter Downloads:** Fixed an issue where certain highly secure websites (like WebNovel and others) would block the app from downloading chapters or loading cover images. The app now handles these secure connections automatically.
* **Cover Art Fixes:** Resolved a visual bug in the Import Modal where some comic covers would appear broken or fail to load. 
* **Home Page Updates:** Adjusted the Home View to correctly display the new discovery feeds and content from all our newly added sources.
* General performance tweaks and under-the-hood stability improvements.
