    Description

    Shen Yuan, handsome and rich, became a second-generation loser and was mercilessly abandoned by his money-worshipping girlfriend after his family’s business shut down. However, because of this, he obtained the [Perfect Life] system. As long as he spent money on the opposite sex with a favorability of 60, he could receive double the amount back. From that moment on, he made full use of the system to win the hearts of beautiful women and earn a fortune. The more he spent, the richer he became. He spent money like water and rose to the top 0.1%, living a happy entrepreneurial life filled with endless passion
